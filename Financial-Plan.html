  • Compound growth without annual tax drag from dividends
  • US tech stocks historically outperform dividend-heavy ASX stocks
  • Tax efficiency: Pay CGT only when you sell (with 50% discount)
  • Reinvested profits drive higher long-term returns

Implementation Timeline

Weeks 1-2: Foundation

Set up brokerage accounts (Stake for US, SelfWealth for ASX). Establish core ETF positions (VGS, IVV, IOO) with 60% of ETF allocation.

Weeks 3-4: Diversification

Add thematic ETFs and begin dollar-cost averaging into crypto positions. Start accumulating stocks on market dips.

Month 2: Optimization

Complete all positions, set up quarterly rebalancing reminders, implement tracking system.

Years 1-3: Accumulation

Hold all positions, reinvest dividends, no selling to avoid CGT. Add to positions if additional funds available.

Years 4-10: Management

Annual review and rebalancing, tax-loss harvesting opportunities, gradual shift if approaching retirement.
